This Weeks Highlighted Artist:

Mihle Lingani

Born in October 2002. I attended Lady Grey Arts Academy in 2017, which as when I was introduced to Design. I spent about a year there learning key structures like design, colour theory and about great Designers & Artists of South Africa. My teacher and the outstanding designers we learnt about at that time greatly inspired me to continue being passionate about design. In 2018 I moved to Cape Town, which unfortunately meant I no longer do Design. Rather for the next 2 years I did Visual Arts at Voortrekker High School

-Graphic Designer, Artist.
